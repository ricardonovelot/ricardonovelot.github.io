About

Since November 2024 I've been a User Experience Designer at Whirlpool, working on the consumer mobile apps for connected laundry and home appliances. Before that I was a product designer at Digital Turbine, where I worked on the MetroZone redesign for T-Mobile and maintained a design system of several hundred UI components. I've also freelanced for RNDVU, a social event planning app.

I studied computer science and audiovisual production at Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León, and that mix still shows in how I work: comfortable in Figma, equally comfortable in Xcode.
